Welcome to the scene shop

The Scene Shop is where the scenic pieces for a staged production or film are constructed and painted. Our shop here at the Schwartz Center includes a dedicated area for Properties fabrication as well. Scenery is considered any constructed units that make up the world of the show or film such as the walls, backdrops and platforms. Properties, also known as props, are anything an actor handles such as weaponry, dishes, oversized banana peels as well as furniture.
